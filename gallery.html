هر که بامش بیش، برفش بیشتر

The taller the roof, the more snow it catches

Big responsibilities come with big positions

دو صد گفته چون نیم کردار نیست

Two hundred words aren't worth half a deed

Actions speak louder than words

سنگ بزرگ نشانه نزدن است

A big stone means you won't throw it

If a goal is too big, you might not even try

درخت هرچه پربارتر، افتاده‌تر

The fuller the tree, the lower it bows

The more humble the truly wise
